Accounting and Finance



Accounting Principles

   Accounting principles are
   the general rules and
   guidelines that companies
   are required to follow when
   reporting all accounts
   and financial data.
   The purpose of following
   accounting principles is
   to communicate economic
   information in a language that
  is acceptable and understandable
  from one business to another.

Management Accounting

Management accounting is the
process of preparing reports
about business operations
that help managers
make short-term and
long-term decisions.
It helps a business pursue
its goals by identifying,
measuring, analyzing,
interpreting and communicating
information to managers

Structure of Accounting

Structure of Accounting
can be divided into five major
categories called
accounts,assets,liabilities,equity,
revenue and expenses.
These topics will help you
better understand what
a chart of accounts is and
how its used by businesses

Practical Accounting

  Practical Accounting provides
 a foundational understanding
 of Accounting and its importance
 within an organization
 and introduces participants
 to basic accounting skills
which are applicable in a
 working accounting environment
